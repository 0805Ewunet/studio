About

Name: Abel Tadiwos

Profile: Artist

Location: Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

I was born in Harar, Ethiopia in 1988 & raised in a vibrant city called Nazareth (Adama), where I attended elementary, high school & university. Since childhood, my interest always has been in painting & drawing. My earliest memories were back in my elementary school, where I was introduced to painting. One of my science teachers used my sketch to teach body parts. Later, when I was in grade 7, the school gave me a chance to paint a large wall mural which led to my first experience in painting. I was recognized and given an award by the schools administration.

After joining Adama University & graduating with a BA in economics in July 2010, I took courses focused on painting & graphic design. This lead me to pursue art full-time and allowing me to spend two years of researching & practicing new techniques. I am inspired by nature and culture. I use both acrylic & oil mediums to paint objects & portraits mostly depicting Ethiopian & African children & women, architecture, and cultural elements. I values and norms. When I am not in the studio, I simply enjoy working with charities that support the wellbeing of children & women.
